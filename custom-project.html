My Spirit Animal

The Fly

The fly is known to ba a dirty annoying animal, however, most people fail to see how resilient a fly is

The fly is most described to have:

  • A pair of functional wings for flight and a pair of vestigial hindwings called halteres for balance
  • three body parts
  • compound eyes

Although flies are known to spread many diseases they have several benefits as well such as:

  1. They are a food source to birds and other insects.
  2. They are agents of decomposition and decaying.
  3. They are responsible for the swahili proverb "nzi kufa kidondani si hasara".
